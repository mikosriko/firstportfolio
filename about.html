Who am I?
As you can read from the home page and the name of the website, my name is Mikolaj Rutyna.
I was born in Poland and raised in the UK for most of my life. Over the years, I have developed an interest in the technical and the creative world. I have a deep passion for video games since childhood, which still resides within me to this day. Not only it fuels my dopamine, but I also gain new ideas and inspiration which is how I mainly ended up here.


So what kind of interests and skills have I developed from “video games”?
• Game development in Roblox (formerly) and Unity.
• Front-end web development.
• Traditional Art:
▸ Landscapes and portraits involving media such as pencils, soft pastels and watercolouring.
• Digital Art:
▸ UI design - for websites and games.
▸ Pixel art - for games.
▸ GFX (Graphic Effects in Blender)
▸ Graphic Design - mainly for videos.
• Video editing:
▸ Stylised editing in a music video fashion.
▸ Trailers


Roblox? Really?
Yup. When I learned to code for the first time with Python at school, I used my knowledge on Roblox which has a built in engine using Lua. Because of Roblox was how I was easily able to transition to Unity due to the same principles in both engines.


What did I do worthwhile?
I took my interest further into Goldsmiths University, which was where I have produced more independent work and able to graduate as a Creative Computerist.
Using my previous work that I am proud of, including my degree, I ended up working my way up the career ladder over the years, which is where I am now. Pretty cool story, eh?
